Taco Recipe

Here's how to make delicious tacos!

First, the ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup corn kernels
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup chopped lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup sliced olives

Next, the instructions

  1. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned.
  2. Drain any excess fat and add the taco seasoning according to the package instructions.
  3. Add the diced tomatoes, black beans, and corn to the skillet. Stir well and let it simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Serve the taco mixture in taco shells or tortillas and top with shredded cheese, sour cream, lettuce, diced tomatoes, and sliced olives.
  5. Enjoy your homemade tacos!
