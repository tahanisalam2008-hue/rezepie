Noodle Recipe

Here's how to make delicious noodles!

First, the ingredients

  • 1 package instant noodles
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup vegetables (carrots, peas, corn)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil

Next, the instructions

  1. Bring water to a boil in a large pot.
  2. Add the instant noodles and cook according to the package instructions.
  3. While the noodles are cooking, whisk the egg in a small bowl.
  4. Once the noodles are done, drain them and return them to the pot.
  5. Add the vegetables and soy sauce to the pot and stir well.
  6. Pour the egg mixture over the noodles and stir until combined.
  7. Drizzle with sesame oil and serve hot.

Enjoy your homemade noodles!
